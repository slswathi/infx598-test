Harvey Spector

A world of 'Suits', the series

Harvey spector is a fictional character from the television series 'Suits.' He is a new York based lawyer, single and smart, believes he must remain top rated lawyer in New York. He doesnt show his emotions out fast, very deep and secretive, but has a deep frienship with his secretary Donna.

Harvey's life changes when he runs past 'Mike', a high school dropout, but extremely talented person with photographic memory. Though Harvey is allowed to hire only Harvard law school graduates, he hires Mike, illegally.

Will Harvey make sure that Mike doesn't end up in prison?
